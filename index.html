About

I’ve always been drawn to technology that feels a little bit like magic. It started with video games, turned into an engineering degree, and somewhere along the way led me to virtual reality and AI.

These days I work in the world of XR and automation — building and experimenting with small, useful AI systems that make everyday work faster and smarter. Things like signal finders, proposal generators, and customer support agents that actually save time instead of creating more of it.

I like solving problems without overcomplicating them. I like talking to people about what they’re building. And if you’re curious about how AI could make your work better, I’d love to chat.
